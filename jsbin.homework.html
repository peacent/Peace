How to prepare Banga soup

This Banga soup is indigenous to Deltans but also loved and enjoyed by people from other states in Nigeria. I so much love this soup because it reminds me of my origin.

Its best served with Starch, Eba and Fufu.

Items needed for Banga soup

  • Meat
  • Freash Prawn
  • Freash Palm Friut
  • Smoked Fish
  • Pepper
  • Bitter Leaf
  • 3 Maggi Cubes
  • Periwinkle
  • Onion
  • Salt
  • Banga Spice

Method used for preparing Banga soup

    😌

  1. Blend all the Banga spices together
  2. Blend pepper
  3. Wash Meat and Smoked fish
  4. Precook meat with Maggi and Salt�
  5. Boil Palm fruit for 20 minutes
  6. Pound the boiled Palm fruit with mortal and pestle�
  7. Wash out extract and place in a pot
  8. Boil the Banga extract for 5 minutes, add the meat, smoked fish, prawn and Periwinkle�
  9. Add the blended spices, onion, pepper, maggi and salt
  10. Add Bitter leaf and Periwinkle
  11. Stir and allow to cook for 3 minutes�

This is how to prepare the popular Deltan Banga soup. Its brownish and red in color and smells so nice. My sumptious Banga soup is the best.
